Gobel Global, established in 2010 has since become a leader in engineering and establishment of energy and network infrastructures world-wide. Brett and Christian Gobel, it’s founders, shared the idea producing clean and economical energy solutions is paramount in a world growing rapidly in population. Beginning with the Southwest Solar project, Gobel Global’s maiden endeavor, began by creating the U.S’s largest solar farm. The GSSA (Great SouthWest Solar Array) is currently the worlds largest photovoltaic network. Spanning 4 states, it is capable of generating 500 million GW of electricity per day, enough to power the entire region.
